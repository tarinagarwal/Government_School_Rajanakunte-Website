About chairperson

Singanayakanahalli Ramaiah Vishwanath

Shri S R Vishwanath is an Indian Politician who is current MLA from Yelahanka constituency since 25 May 2008. He is former Chairman of Bangalore Development Authority and presently Member of Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) Board. He is a social worker and the chairperson of Government school and PU college of Rajanukunte. He is the founder of Vishwa Vani Foundation.
